Winston Churchill

"If your going through hell, keep going" - Winston Churchill

Winston Churchill was born in 1874. Why was he so important? He saved Britain! Winston was a political figure. He became a member of parliament in 1900 and became prime minister of Britain. He was known to give powerful and inspiring speeches. This would prove paramount for what was to come.

World War II, Winston saw Hitler as a threat before most members of parliament. Hitler waged war on the world, he was successfully swallowing up most of Europe until he reached Britain’s doorstep. Nazi’s were running many bombing raids on Britain. Parliament was heading for a truce, but Winston was not one to give in. Nazi’s were running many successful bombing missions on Britain and the Royal Air Fleet were struggling to defend. When parliament was ready to make a truce Winston was outraged.
